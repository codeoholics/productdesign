VIRTUAL ART GALLERY

A virtual platform that replicates the experience of visiting an art gallery, allowing users to explore and appreciate artworks from the comfort of their homes. The Virtual Art Gallery Experience can include 3D renditions of famous art galleries, allowing users to navigate through rooms and view high-resolution images of artworks. Implement features such as audio guides, artist biographies, and interactive elements to enhance the user experience. Artists could also have the option to showcase and sell their work through the platform. This project aims to bring art enthusiasts closer to the art world, fostering creativity and cultural appreciation in a digital environment.
